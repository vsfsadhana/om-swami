    Om Swami

    Life and Journey

    Om Swami was a multi-millionaire by the time he was twenty-five. But at thirty, he gave away his wealth to embark on his spiritual journey. From

    penthouses in Sydney and London to living in a mud-hut with no running water or flushing cistern, in terrifying Himalayan solitude, his is not quite the usual story we are used to.

    1979
    Swami’s
    birth

    Born on 30-Nov-1979 in Punjab, India, and youngest of the three siblings Om Swami was a quiet child. His mum still has the tiffin you see him holding in this pic.

    1987
    First
    Prize

    Om Swami receiving his first ever academic award for scoring the highest marks in grade 2. "It was a pencil case, I still remember" he says. "By the way, I never liked course books.

    1992
    Practicing
    the Vedas

    When 11-yr-old, he learned Vedic chanting and astrology. A 13-yr-old Swami just after finishing a yajna at his home. The tiffin seen here is the same one he held in the first pic.

    1998
    Waltzing
    Matilda

    Om Swami receiving his first ever academic award for scoring the highest marks in grade 2. "It was a pencil case, I still remember" he says. "By the way, I never liked course books.

    2000
    First
    Car

    Swami's reputation in e-business was only preceded by his impeccable delivery record. At 20, already a CTO, he bought his first car to welcome his visiting mother. A SAAB convertible.

    2002
    Flourishing
    Business

    With a B.Bus from UWS (2000) and an MBA from UTS (2002), Swami began growing the software company he'd started a year earlier. With his best friend, David Soo, and brother, Rajan Sharma.

    2005
    A multi-millionaire

    By the time he was 25, Om Swami had expanded his software company in five countries. His pastimes: badminton, chess and software. A 25-yr-old Swami with his Porsche Carerra 4S.

    2007
    Incredible
    India

    Swami exited software, moved back to India, and built another profitable business, Bloom. Later acquired by the iconic conglomerate, Future Group. Pic: press conference, May 2008.

    2010
    The
    Renunciation

    In Mar 2010, Om Swami gave away his wealth and disappeared. He meditated for thirteen months in the Himalayas in intense solitude. Pic: two days after he concluded his main sadhana.

    2011
    Badrika
    Ashram

    Om Swami set up Sri Badrika Ashram in Himachal Pradesh, nestled in a beautiful valley, next to Giri Ganga. Pic: descending the steps to the ashram temple with his personal aides.

    2021
    Present
    Day

    Every year, Om Swami usually spends seven months in solitude and four in the ashram giving discourses and meeting people. He rarely gives media interviews or speaks at external events.
