I'm not here to impress anyone and do not believe in self-abnegation or lifelong austerity. One ought to be flexible. It's perfectly fine if you disagree with me. Please don't follow me or the idea of me. I make my decisions independently of how the world might take them. I encourage you to do the same. What is the point of liberation, otherwise?
